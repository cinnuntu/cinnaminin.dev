StowableXP

Withdraw and Store your XP in Bottles and Books with ease.

About

StowableXP is a Minecraft server plugin that lets players store their XP safely inside bottles and books.

Commands

  • /bottlexp – Store XP into bottles.
  • /bookxp – Store XP inside books.

Features

  • Configurable XP rates
  • Lightweight and efficient
  • Easy to install
